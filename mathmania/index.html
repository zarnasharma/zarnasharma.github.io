Math Mania Introduction

Welcome to Math Mania !

Math Mania is a simple boardgame that also assesses your basic arithmetic skills. Play this game for fun with your friends, at work, or anywhere at all.

Game Instructions

  1. The total game duration is 2 minutes.
  2. Roll the dice to begin the game.
  3. Choose the correct answer to move ahead in the game.
  4. Every correct answer allows you to progress ahead in the game.
  5. Every incorrect answer decrements the attempts left.
  6. There are 20 levels to clear in 2 minutes.
  7. You only have 3 chances to finish the game.

So what are you waiting for? Let's get started !

Warning: This game is freakishly addictive. Ask the developer about it!
